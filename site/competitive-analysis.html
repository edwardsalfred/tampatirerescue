Patterns of the top 10%

Six moves that separate leaders from templates.

01

The phone number lives everywhere.

Top sites repeat it in nav, hero, footer, and a sticky mobile bar. Weak sites bury it in a contact form. The rebuild treats the dial-pad as the product.

02

Pricing transparency is a moat.

Only one competitor publishes rates. That single decision is their strongest trust signal. Publishing baseline pricing on the rebuild's Services page captures the same trust on day one.

03

Real testimonials beat fake stars.

Named first-name reviews with specific scenarios outperform generic five-star icons. The current site has one quote. The rebuild ships with four to six.

04

A specific time promise wins.

Tampa Tire Rescue already claims a 20-minute response. Every other competitor says "fast." The rebuild promotes that number to a dominant headline.

05

Per-city content is SEO gold.

Fourteen cities served means fourteen long-tail keywords. The rebuild creates indexable per-city anchor sections (and optional per-city pages) every competitor lacks.

06

None of them have ambition with design.

Every Tampa-market site is a template. A scroll-animated, custom-typed, image-rich build immediately reads as the legitimate option in the city — before the visitor reads a word.

Recommended direction

The design system, justified by the data.

Palette

Asphalt black #0B0B0D, hazard amber #FFC400, rescue red #E63946. Roadside-rescue signal language. Distinct from every competitor's blue or template-default scheme.

Typography

Bangers (display, all-caps italic) for hero and section headlines — mirrors the existing wordmark and is louder than any competitor type. Inter (400/500/700) for body. JetBrains Mono for phone numbers and the response-time stat block.

Animation

GSAP + ScrollTrigger throughout. Pinned hero with a marked placeholder for a 3D scroll-stop video asset (to be generated separately). Reveal-on-scroll for service cards, parallax on imagery, dial-pulse on the sticky phone bar.

Content moves not seen anywhere in market

  • Headline-scale "20 min" response-time number, animated count-up from 0.
  • Live "Now Open" status indicator (pulse dot).
  • Map-driven service-area visualization with the 14 cities tagged.
  • Six-step "How it works" diagram (call → dispatch → arrive → diagnose → repair/replace → drive away).
  • Pricing transparency block — flat baseline + add-ons.
  • FAQ block with schema markup.
